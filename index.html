Minevancic



Rules

  • Go from the lower left corner to the upper right corner without stepping on any mines.
  • Be careful with the time! If time runs out, you lose.
  • You cannot go back, if you go back you will lose 20 points
  • There will be hidden mines!
  • The number of mines will increase depending on the level.
  • If the mine is 3 or more squares away Rover will turn green.
  • If the mine is 2 squares away, Rover will turn yellow.
  • If the mine is 1 Rover square away it will turn red.
  • You can ask for help, but 100 points will be deducted
